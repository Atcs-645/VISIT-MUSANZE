Your dream destinations in Musanze

Virunga National Park

"Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Virunga National Park, home to the majestic mountain gorillas and stunning volcanoes. Experience unforgettable treks and immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity of this UNESCO World Heritage site."

Bisate Hikes

"Embark on an exhilarating adventure with Bisate Hikes, where you can trek through lush forests and witness the beauty of Rwanda's landscapes. Experience the thrill of hiking in the heart of nature and discover the unique flora and fauna that call this region home."

Lake Ruhondo

"Discover the serene beauty of Lake Ruhondo, a hidden gem surrounded by lush hills and vibrant wildlife. Enjoy boat rides, fishing, and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and adventure."

Dian Fossey

"Visit the Dian Fossey Gorilla Fund, dedicated to the conservation of mountain gorillas. Learn about the incredible work of Dian Fossey and her legacy in protecting these magnificent creatures while exploring the stunning landscapes of Volcanoes National Park."

Red Rocks Arts

"Experience the vibrant culture of Rwanda at Red Rocks Arts, where you can engage with local artists and artisans. Discover traditional crafts, art exhibitions, and cultural performances that showcase the rich heritage of the region."

Musanze City

Musanze, formerly known as Ruhengeri, is a vibrant town and tourism hub in the Northern Province of Rwanda, known as the "gateway" to Volcanoes National Park and the Virunga Mountains, offering a unique experience for both nature and culture enthusiasts.

Musanze Caves

The Musanze Cave is 2km long and the most frequently visited. It is on the Innes University grounds and lies in the volcanic region dating back 65 million years
